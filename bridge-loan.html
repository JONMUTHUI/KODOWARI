Bridge Loan

Bridge Loan is meant for improvements including the purchase of lab computer equipment, classroom furniture, smart classes, CCTV installation, construction of restrooms, advance for teachers’ salaries , renovations and more.

Features

  • From to KES 200,000 – 500,000.
  • Convenient, doorstep service.
  • Fast processing and simple documentation.
  • Loan disbursement through RTGS.

    • Eligibility

  • Be fully registered with the Ministry of Education.
  • Institution should be 3 years old and over
  • Minimum student enrolment of 250.
  • Strong operational income and Committed Management.
  • Good repayment track of existing loans (if any).
